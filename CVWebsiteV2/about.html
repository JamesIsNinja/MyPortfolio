{About James}

As you’ve likely already figured out, I’m James White. Some might consider me to be a nerd, a programmer in training by profession, as well as amateur graphics designer, server host, video gamer, PC hardware enthusiast, sports lover, motorcyclist and more!

I’ve had technology in my hands since I was old enough to hold any! I’ve been playing video games since before I can even remember, given an SNES to keep me preoccupied for a while, the SNES turned into a Gameboy, and that into an N64, into a PS1, and so on and so forth. I have been on the leading edge of consumer technology for most of my life, early adoption of the smart watch as the most recent example.

I’m an avid supporter of advancing technology and supporting new technologies and theories, whether it be hardware, or software. I love seeing where we’ve come from and where technology is headed. Technology we can wear, interact with everywhere in our lives, things we used to see in movies becoming reality is so mind blowing to me, and I want to be a part of it.

In my personal life aside from video games, I enjoy watching sports NFL, and NHL for the most part, but most sporting events are fun. I also love motorcycling, I’ve been riding dirt bikes since I was 8, and been on the back of a motorcycle since I was even younger, got my motorcycle license right at 16 and have been riding on and off since, I enjoy it for the freedom, and different feeling you get then driving a car. It’s much more thrilling. I also enjoy building and tinkering with PCs, tech hardware is very interesting to me.

I have been, and always will be hungry for tech knowledge, and furthering my experience in, and with it. That is pretty much me in a nutshell.
